    • Welcome to SchöneMöbel

    Welcome to SchöneMöbel At SchöneMöbel, we're passionate about creating beautiful, functional, and sustainable furniture that brings joy to your home. Our journey began , "Established in 2015, SchöneMöbel began as a small online marketplace for handmade, sustainable furniture crafted by local artisans. Since then, we've grown into a premier destination for stylish, eco-friendly furniture solutions, partnering with renowned designers and suppliers to bring you the best selection of high-quality pieces and since then, we've been dedicated to crafting pieces that reflect our values of quality, comfort, and style.

    Our Story

    As a family-owned business, we understand the importance of creating spaces that bring people together. We believe that a well-designed home is not just a reflection of personal taste, but also a reflection of the memories and moments shared within its walls. That's why we're committed to using only the finest materials, sustainable practices, and timeless designs to create furniture that stands the test of time.

    What Sets Us Apart

    We're proud to stand out from the crowd by: Using high-quality, eco-friendly materials whenever possible Partnering with local artisans and suppliers to support the community Offering customization options to fit your unique style Providing exceptional customer service and warranty support
